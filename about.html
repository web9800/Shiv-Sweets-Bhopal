The Beginning

A Sweet Begining In The Heart Of Bhopal

Founded in 1985 by Shri Shiv Kumar Sharma, Shiv Sweets began as a small boutique sweet shop in the bustling streets of Bhopal. With a simple promise of purity and a passion for authentic flavors, we have grown into a beloved destination for sweet lovers across the city.

Every piece of mithai we craft tells a story of heritage. We use only the finest ingredients—pure desi ghee, handpicked kaju from the South, and fresh milk from local dairies. Today, while we embrace modern techniques and global standards, our heart remains rooted in the traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Our Core Values
